## Naming rules

### Do

- **Sovereign Browser** — client-facing product, installers, marketing
- **Unykorn Sovereign Browser** — enterprise contracts, legal headers
- **Powered by Sovereign** — white-label co-brand footer
- **Storm Ops** — operator dashboard at `storm.unykorn.org` only
- **DONK** — agent side panel, voice persona, mesh references

### Do not

- Call the browser product **Storm Browser** (retired)
- Imply **Google Chrome** endorsement — we ship Chromium/CEF under our brand
- Use **Unykorn** as a verb or generic term for "any AI browser"
- Mix **Storm Ops** branding into client white-label installers
- Register client namespaces without SNP policy review

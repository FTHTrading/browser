## Positioning

**Sovereign Browser is the installable agentic browser your clients brand as their own — with namespace identity, provenance, compliance, and pay-per-action built in, not bolted on.**

**Not sold as:** storm.unykorn.org access, a Chrome extension, or "AI sidebar for your website."

## ICP (ideal customer profiles)

| Segment | Pain | Sovereign offer |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Content creators / authors** | AI scrapes work; no proof of origin | LPS-1 + Creator Studio + DOI corpus RAG |
| **Web3 / NFT / token projects** | Wallet + dapp friction | SNP + x402 + Apostle receipts |
| **Agencies (white-label)** | Clients want "our browser" | Build pipeline + rev share |
| **Regulated fintech / RWA** | Can't use Comet (cloud exfil) | Enterprise: law.unykorn + air-gapped gateway |
